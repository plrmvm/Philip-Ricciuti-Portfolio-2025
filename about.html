ABOUT.

ARTIST STATEMENT

Philip Ricciuti is a New York City–based 3D designer and street photographer with a degree in 3D Digital Design from the Rochester Institute of Technology and a minor in Jewelry Design.

With a lifelong, obsessive drive to create, his work blends product visualization, lighting, industrial design, and photorealistic rendering into striking visual narratives.

Proficient in tools like Blender, Houdini, Maya, Substance Painter, Unreal Engine 5, and the Adobe Creative Suite, Philip brings both technical precision and artistic intent to his projects.

Outside the studio, he’s a dedicated street photographer with a prolific catalog, constantly shooting and creating as part of his daily rhythm.
